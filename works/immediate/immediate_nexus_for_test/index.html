Investing in crypto comes with its set of risks. While not certainties, the following are potential challenges investors may face, highlighting common concerns in the market.

  • Market volatility can lead to significant gains or losses
  • Security risks associated with data management by unreliable entities
  • Market fluctuations and limited regulatory oversight globally
  • Restricted mainstream adoption in daily transactions
  • Market fragmentation, posing learning and decision-making challenges for newcomers
